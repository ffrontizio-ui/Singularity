SINGULARITY PROTOCOL DOCUMENTATION // V1.0

1. What is Singularity?

Singularity is a Darknet microblogging platform and onion link directory built entirely on the principle of Zero-Trust and decentralized identity. The platform contains zero user databases, requires no emails or passwords, and uses no tracking telemetry. Your identity here is purely a set of cryptographic keys.

2. Identity & Crypto

To begin transmitting, you don't need a traditional account. Follow these steps:

  • Generate Identity: Navigate to the Generate Identity page. The system will give you a 15-word Mnemonic Seed.
  • Secure Your Seed: These 15 words are your absolute and only private key. Losing them means losing your identity forever. There is no "Forgot Password" feature because we don't own your data.
  • Public Fingerprint: When you publish a post, the grid only sees your Public Key. It's an irreversible alphanumeric string that proves grid ownership without compromising your anonymity.

3. Data Transmission (Proof of Work)

To prevent spam bots from flooding the grid, we replace privacy-invasive Captchas with a cryptographic Proof of Work (PoW) system.

  • When drafting a post on the Transmit Post page, your browser will silently compute a complex mathematical hash (Nonce). This takes a few seconds before the submit button unlocks.
  • Self-Destruct Protocol: You can dictate your post's survival time (1 Hour, 24 Hours, or 7 Days). Once the timer hits zero, the node permanently shreds your text and attached images from the database.
  • Secure Attachments: Any image you upload goes through an automated filter that strips all EXIF metadata (which could leak GPS coordinates or camera models) before saving it purely as safe pixel data.

4. Darknet Directory & Voting

The platform features a smart Onion Link index accessible at the Darknet Directory. This is heavily driven by community intelligence:

  • Automated Verification: When you contribute a new .onion link, the server pings it via the Tor network in the background. Dead links are labeled OFFLINE until they respond.
  • Auto-Mirrors: If you try to visit an offline target, the system dynamically hunts for an active mirror with identical taxonomy and stealthily reroutes your connection.
  • Intermediary Security Layer (Preview Service): Accessing an indexed link throws you into a sandbox preview screen. This screen actively scans the target's HTTP headers and scrapes PGP public keys to prevent phishing attacks.
  • Decentralized Voting: You can Upvote or Downvote platforms. Votes are guarded by rotating Server-Side Hashes to prevent brute-forcing without logging IP addresses. Downvoted thresholds auto-label grids as "HIGH RISK / SCAM."

5. Extreme Privacy Guidelines

NO-JS MODE PROTOCOLS

Running the Tor Browser on 'Safest' (Disabling Javascript) is partially supported by the architecture:

  1. To transmit posts or directory links without JS, you must manually run the `pow_solver.py` script locally on an air-gapped machine and manually inject the `nonce` via CURL or raw HTTP requests.
  2. The Tor Browser is the ONLY supported avenue for traversing this grid securely.
  3. Never leak your 15-word mnemonic phrase. Anyone possessing it cryptographically becomes you.

6. Personal Bitcoin Addresses (Tips)

Every identity on Singularity is automatically mapped to a unique Bitcoin (BTC) address.

  • Deterministic Mapping: Your 15-word seed derivatively generates your Bitcoin address. This address is persistent; it will never change for your identity.
  • Native Segwit: We use the modern `bc1q` (Bech32) format, ensuring minimal transaction fees and maximum compatibility with privacy-focused wallets.

7. Data Retrieval (Export)

It is your absolute right to reclaim your data footprints. From the Export My Grid page, enter your cryptographic seed to download an unadulterated JSON ledger containing your historic transmissions and digital signatures.
